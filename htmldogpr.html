Strong for the Homeless

C. Strong Mission to Help the Peninsulas Homeless

With the recent and on going pandemic there has been a rise in homelessness that has put a strain on the already taxed social system in Hampton Roads. C. Strong Inc hopes to combat the food insecurity of the homeless by partnering with various local restaurants to provide food to the homeless. Attached is a Resume of past experiences that have inspired me to create this non-profit organization.
